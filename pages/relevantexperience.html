Other Work Experience

Hospital Volunteer

Stony Brook University Medical Center

As a volunteer in the Mother-Baby Unit, I assisted parents through the process of applying their newborn for social security and receiving their birth certificate. I also checked on patients periodically to see if they needed anything (comfort rounding). Additionally, I made sure all newborns received their appropriate vaccinations prior to leaving the hospital.
